Bills at Delta House of Assembly

  1. Prohibition of Misuse of Academic, Professional and Traditional Tittles, Bill 2003.
  2. Appropriation Bill, 2004 Bill 2003.
  3. Traditional Rulers Council and Chiefs (Amendment) Bill, of Delta State, 2003 (HB. 19)

  6. Development Administration and Committee, Bills 2004 (HB. 22)
  7. Local Government (Amendment) Bill, 2004 (HB. 25)
  8. Regulations of Private Veterinary Clinics, Bills 2003 (HB. 27).
  9. Delta State House of Assembly Commission (Amendment), Bill 2004 (HB. 28)
  10. Local Government Bill of Delta State of Nigeria (HB. 29)
