International Conferences Attended
In spite of his sound educational pedigree, Hon. Ossai Nicholas Ossai sees knowledge in any form as worth the while. He attended several conferences but not limited to:
  1. The New World Order and its Implication on Africa at the third African Regional Conference on Sustainable Development, Owerri, Nigeria.
  2. The Elite and Sustenance of Democratic Governance in Nigeria – 1999 – 2007” for the Association of third World Studies 27th conference – Nov. 2009
  3. World summit of Legislators held in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil 2012
