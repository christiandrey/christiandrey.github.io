His Education

Hon. Ossai Nicholas Ossai started his educational sojourn at the Urban Community Primary School, Abakiliki, Ebony State. He proceeded to CMS Grammar School, Lagos for his secondary education. Armed with his West African School Certificate, (WASC) he proceeded to the University of Nigeria Nsukka where he obtained a Bachelor degree in Agriculture, Majoring in Agricultural Economics in 1996. Not done, he went back to the same University and obtained a Post Graduate Diploma in Public Administration in 2002.

Again, the quest for knowledge took him to the University of Benin, Edo State where he obtained a Masters degree in Business Administration in 2004. Also in 2005, he obtained from the University of Nigeria Nsukka, a Masters Degree in Public Administration. Just recently, he successfully defended his dissertation for a PhD in Public Administration from University of Nigeria Nsukka. So, he can be rightly addressed as Hon. Rev. Dr. Ossai Nicholas Ossai.

Membership of Professional Bodies

Hon. Ossai N Ossai believes that professionalism is the bedrock for excellent performance in any sphere of human endeavour. Thus, in 2007 he became Chartered Mediator. He also went ahead in 2008 to become a member, Projects Management Professionals of Nigeria.
