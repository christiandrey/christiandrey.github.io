Bills sponsored at The House of Representatives

Despite his late entry into the House of Representatives, Hon. Ossai N. Ossai was able to settle down for serious business. He has sponsored very important Bills as follows:

  1. A Bill for an Act to Prohibit Movement from 6am to 6pm of Certain Articulated Vehicles on Nigerian Road, and to provide for other related matters (Has passed second reading, now in Committee stage). 2012.
  2. A Bill for an Act to Amend the FCT Customary Court Act, Cap.F39, LFN, 2007. (Has passed second Reading, now at the Committee stage). 2012.
  3. A Bill for an Act to Alter Provisions of item No.1, Part iii, Third Schedule of the Constitution of the Federal Republic of Nigeria (As Amended) 1990, to allow for Additional Membership of the FCT Judicial Service Committee, to include the Customary Court to the list of Courts in the FCT and other matters connected therewith. (Has passed second reading, now at the Committee stage) 2012.
  4. A Bill for an Act to Establish the Nigerian Institute of Social Work and regulate the practice of Professional Social Work in Nigeria and for other related matters. (Has passed through second reading, now at the Committee stage).
  5. A Bill for an Act to review and standardize the penalties created in certain acts of parliament and for other related matters (Has passed first reading).
  6. A Bill for an Act to Amend the Federal Lands Registry (Miscellaneous Provisions) Act, CAP F15, 1992 and for other matters connected thereto- (Committee stage 2013)
  7. A Bill for an Act to Alter the Constitution of the Federal Republic of Nigeria 1999 as Amended and for other related matters (Second Reading)
  8. A bill for an Act to Amend the Federal Capital Territory customary Court Act, CAP F37, LFN, 2007 (second Reading)
Motions at The House of Representatives

Hon. Ossai N. Ossai moved the following motions at The House of Representatives

  1. Abandonment of the Kwale-Ogume-Amai-Umuebu-Obiaruku-Ebedei-Umutu-Agbor Federal Highway (2012)
  2. Disruption of Scanning Machines and other Security Gadgets in our Airports (2012)
  3. Recovery of Revenue due to the Federation Account from NNPC and its Joint Venture Partners. (2012)
  4. The need for Mr. President to enforce the Resolution of the House of Representatives on the investigation into the near collapse of the Capital Market. (2012)
  5. Flood devastates Communities in Ndokwa/Ukwuani and Isoko Federal Constituencies of Delta States. (2012)
  6. Negating the impeachment of Kogi State Speaker. (2012)
  7. Need to implement House Resolutions on the Report of Investigation on the crash of Dana and Allied Aircrafts. (2012)
  8. Brutal killing of ten indigenes of Ogume community by unknown herdsmen in Delta state (2013)
  9. Making the Nigeria Agip Oil (NAOC) to pay compensations to 25 oil spillage impacted communities of Ndokwa East Local Government Area of Delta State (2013)
  10. Need to review the payment of N500 Monthly Service Charge in the New Electricity Tariff of PHCN (2013)
  11. Motion on the Need for Sewer and Water Supply Systems in Apo-New Garki Town and other Estates within Abuja.
  12. Thunderstorm devastates communities in Ndokwa/Ukwuani Federal Constituency (2014)
  13. Need for a thorough investigation into the implementation and management of the fund constituted under the tertiary education trust fund (2014)
  14. Fallout from the ill-fated immigration recruitment exercise.
