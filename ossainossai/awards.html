Honorary Mentions/Merit Awards

In recognition of his contributions to the development of our society, Hon.Ossai Nicholas Ossai has received several awards from organisations both within and outside Nigeria. Some of these awards are:

  1. Distinguished Service Award; by Niger Delta Peoples Network at Nelrose Hotel on 26th August, 2003
  2. Distinguished Service Award; by True Talk Foundation at Nelrose Hotel, Asaba on 28th July, 2004
  3. Distinguished Citizen Award of Utagba-Uno; by the Utagba-Uno Community at Ndueze Primary School – Utagba-Uno June, 2004
  4. Award of Friend of Education 2004 by Nigeria Students League at Federal College of Education Okene on 16th February 2005
  5. Honorary Citizens of Louisiana (USA) 2001
  6. Distinguished and Meritorious Service Award; by Faculty of Agriculture University of Nigeria, Nsukka at Princess Alexandra Auditorium UNN on 25th January, 2005
  7. 2005 Most Outstanding Law Maker Award; by PDP National Grassroot Forum at Adamawa Hall Nicon Nuga Hotel Abuja on 24th February 2005
  8. Distinguished Service Award; by Igbo Students Renaissance at Hotel Presidential Enugu, on 11th March 2005
  9. Excellency Award – As the Best Legislator of the year 2004 Ndokwa West ever produced by frontline news media at House of Assembly Complex Asaba on 8th March, 2005
  10. Distinguished Leadership Award; by National Association of Nigeria Student (NANS) Executive Council October, 2006)
  11. Man of the year, Award by Niger Delta Achievers Merit Award, at Delta Council of Art and Culture Pavilion Asaba 2005
  12. Vocational Service Award by Rotary Club, Asaba at Maduemezia Plaza, Asaba 2005
  13. Paul Harris Fellow Award by Rotary International at Enotel Hotel, Asaba 2005
  14. Award of Philanthropic Gesture and best legislator of Ndokwa nation, 2005 by Ndokwa Vanguard at Jaros Hotel, Kwale 2005
  15. Award of Excellence by National Association of Delta State Students Ambrose Ali University, Ekpoma at House of Assembly Complex, Asaba 2007
  16. The most Labour friendly Administrator awarded by the academic staff union and non-academic staff union of Delta State College of Education, Agbor 2009.
  17. Worthy Ambassador to Delta State House of Assembly by the Delta State House of Assembly, Asaba 26th June, 2012.
  18. Award for Excellent Performance by Umutu Development Union in Delta State 2012.
  19. Award of Excellence on Political Leadership by the department of Public Administration, Faculty of Social sciences, University of Nigeria, Nsukka. 2012.
  20. Award of Executive Leadership and Performance Management by Howard University School of Business, Washington DC, USA. 2012.
