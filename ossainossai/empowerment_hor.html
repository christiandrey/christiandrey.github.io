Empowerment projects executed at the House of Representatives

In his determination to attract Federal presence to his Constituency, Hon. Ossai Nicholas Ossai attracted, according to his peoples’ critical needs the following projects:

Water supply

In 2012, he attracted hand pump boreholes to the following villages:

  1. Ukwuani LGA (Isemelu, Ezionum, Eziokpor)
  2. Ndokwa West (Emu Uno, Ugili Amai, Ogume)
  3. Ndokwa East (Ossissa, Ushie, Ibrede, Afor)

In 2013 he provided the following solar Powered Water Schemes at the following locations to further tackle the water need of his people:

  1. Okpai Obieze – on-going
  2. Amorji Onicha Ukwuani - on-going
  3. Umuono Ossissa - on-going
  4. Obi Obeti
  5. Umuebu in 2013 - on-going
Health

He has also made significant landmarks in making sure that healthcare delivery is improved in his Constituency. In 2012, two Primary Healthcare Centres were attracted at the following locations:

  1. Iyede Amei
  2. Aballa Oshimili

He has also attracted Additional Comprehensive Healthcare Hospitals provided for in 2013 budget in the following locations thus:

  1. Ogume
  2. Aballa Uno
  3. Akoku Uno

To strengthen the existing health facilities across his constituency, he has earmarked 10 Health Centres to benefit from the already procured medical eqiupmet/supplies. Hon. Ossai Nicholas Ossai in 2013 collaborated with Ndokwa Association, Atlanta Georgia, USA and donated hospital equipment shared across the hospitals in his constituency.

Electrification

Again in 2012 and 2013, he embarked on the following electricity projects:

  1. 1 unit of 500kva transformer was supplied to Kwale.
  2. Provision of Solar Powered Streetlights at Kwale.
  3. Provision of Solar Powered Streetlights at Ogume.
  4. He provided Solar Streetlights in Onuogbokor, Eweshi, Ebedei, Ulogwe Isumpe, Ndemili, Amorji, Eziokpor, Asaba Ase and Ase.
Education
  1. Supply of 120 pieces of computers for ICT at Ibrede Grammar School, Ibrede
  2. Supply of 220 pieces of chairs/desk to Utagba Ogbe Grammar School Kwale.
Roads
  1. Major repairs of Kwale Township Road from Ogume junction to Eke market.
  2. Major repairs of Kwale-Ogume-Amai-Umuebu-Ebedei-Obiaruku Road.
Poverty Eradication/Grassroot Empowerment Schemes

Hon. Ossai Nicholas Ossai sees the empowerment of his constituents as paramount. To this end he has distributed the following items to his people:

  1. 10 units of cars
  2. 36 units of keke NAPEP
  3. 70 units of grinding Machines
  4. 40 units of motorcycles
  5. 30 units of generators.

It is noteworthy to state that in the spirit of sharing with the privileged and the less privileged in his constituency, Hon. Ossai Nicholas Ossai shares rice and other items that go with festivities at the end of every year.
