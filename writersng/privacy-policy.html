Revision Policy

We are always ready to revise your paper if it does not entirely meet your expectations. We will revise your paper at no cost if your request is in line with our terms:

  1. Your revision directions should remain the same and not conflict with your initial specifications.
  2. You must submit your request for revision using the request revision button on your personal order page.
  3. You can request a revision at any time before approving your order, but you have only 7 days after the order has been approved to request a revision.

If you require significant changes to the original order or request a revision 7 days after an order has been approved, you will have to pay an extra fee. Contact our admin for an estimate.

For orders with the Progressive Delivery feature (longer than 20pages and delivered in batches as agreed upon by writer and customer- (most frequently full Thesis or Dissertation orders) the deadline for a revision request is limited to 21 days after initial approval.

Please note that we hold the rights to extend the deadline for revision as per the writer’s request in cases where the delivery time frame you have initially paid for allows such extension.

When requesting a different writer, please remember that additional time is needed to find a new writer, so make sure you adjust your revision deadline accordingly. We suggest you add an extra 24 hours to the initial deadline for the new writer.
