1. You are only allowed to vote once.

2. You MUST vote for one candidate per position.

3. To vote, please click on the box that has the picture of your choice of candidate and his/her name.

4. Make use of the Previous and Next Buttons to navigate between available positions to vote.

5. When you have finished voting, click on the ‘Submit votes’ button at the top right hand corner to submit your votes. Failure to do so will nullify your votes.

6. You will be automatically logged out after submitting.
